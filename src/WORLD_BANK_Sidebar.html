About the World Bank

The World Bank is an international financial institution that provides loans, grants, and technical assistance to developing countries. It maintains one of the most comprehensive collections of development data in the world, covering over 1,400 indicators for 200+ economies.

World Bank Indicators API

This add-on uses the World Bank Indicators API v2 to query data directly into your spreadsheet. The API is free, open, and requires no authentication. Data is available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 license.

Tips

💡 Presets — Use the indicator preset dropdown in the Explorer tab to quickly load common indicator bundles like Economic Basics or Demographics.
💡 Country groups — Click G7, BRICS, EU, or other group buttons to select countries in bulk. Combine with region or income filters for precise selection.
💡 Wide formats — Choose "Wide (years as columns)" for easy charting in Sheets. Use "Wide (indicators as columns)" to compare multiple indicators side by side.
💡 Custom functions — Type =WB_DATA("SP.POP.TOTL","USA",2000,2023) directly into a cell for quick lookups without opening the sidebar.
