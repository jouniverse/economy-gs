World Trade Organization

The WTO is the global trade body for negotiated rules and trade statistics. This add-on reads WTO statistical series via the Timeseries API and writes results into your sheet for further analysis in Google Sheets.

WTO Timeseries API

Uses API v1. Register at the API Portal for a key. Rate limits apply (about 1 request/second for /data and /data_count). Many series cover years from 1948 onward when data exist (not every indicator has full history).

MFN and tariffs (Quick Fetch)

MFN (most-favoured-nation) is the non-discriminatory tariff treatment WTO members must extend to imports from any other member (subject to exceptions such as preferential trade agreements). Indicator TP_A_0010 is the simple average MFN applied tariff across all products: it is a summary measure of how high applied tariffs are on average (not merchandise trade volumes). Higher values generally mean higher protection. Use it for economy-wide comparisons of tariff levels, not for trade value flows.
